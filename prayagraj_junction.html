PrayagRaj Junction....

Prayagraj, formerly known as Allahabad, is a city steeped in history, spirituality, and cultural richness. At the heart of this vibrant city is the confluence of the Ganges, Yamuna, and the mythical Saraswati rivers, a sacred site known as the Triveni Sangam. This intersection of rivers is believed to possess immense spiritual power and hosts the grand Kumbh Mela, the largest human gathering on Earth. Prayagraj's spirituality is further exemplified by its numerous temples, such as the Mankameshwar Temple and the Alopi Devi Temple. For a more contemporary experience, the New Yamuna Bridge, which lights up in the evening, offers a captivating view.

Explore The City of Sangam

Nestled along the sacred confluence of the Ganges, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers, Prayagraj invites you on a journey of a lifetime. This city, steeped in history, is more than a mere destination; it's a spiritual odyssey. At the Triveni Sangam, where these rivers merge, pilgrims and seekers from across the globe come to cleanse their souls and partake in the age-old rituals. Prayagraj is not just a city; it's a kaleidoscope of faith, history, and culture. It's an open invitation to explore the profound and revel in the extraordinary. Come, let Prayagraj's stories etch themselves into your heart, for this is a journey you'll cherish forever.

One District One Product(Moonj Products)

The Naini area of Prayagraj is known for its Moonj craft. Easy availability of raw materials has enabled this craft to flourish in the district. The market is flooded with a variety of products made from moonj like basket (daliya), coaster stand, bags, decorative items and more. Eco-friendly moonj products have the potential to do well in the national as well as international markets.
